Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: [[10 02 2025]]

This cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product/service purchased through the Platform.

1. Cancellations

Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within [[30]] days of placing the order. However, cancellation requests may not be entertained if:

  • The order has already been communicated to the seller/merchant and shipping has been initiated.
  • The product is already out for delivery (in such cases, you may reject the product at the doorstep).
  • The order contains perishable items like flowers, eatables, etc.

2. Damaged / Defective Items

In case of damaged or defective items, please report to our customer service team within [[30]] days of receipt. The request will be entertained once the seller/merchant has verified the issue.

3. Product Not as Expected

If you feel the product is not as described or does not meet expectations, report this within [[30]] days of receiving the product. The customer service team will review and take an appropriate decision.

4. Manufacturer Warranty

For products with manufacturer warranty, complaints should be directed to the respective manufacturer as per their policy.

5. Refunds

In case of refunds approved by [[foundris welfare foundation]], it will take [[30]] days for the refund to be processed to you.
