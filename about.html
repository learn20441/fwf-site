About FWF

Empowering communities with skills, support, and sustainable opportunities.

Our Story

Foundris Welfare Foundation (FWF) was born with a vision to transform livelihoods. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to grow, contribute, and thrive. Through skill-based training, micro-enterprise support, and strong community partnerships, FWF works to create a future where sustainable income and dignity of labor are accessible to all.

Our Mission

To support individuals by providing skill training, start-up assistance, and market linkages that enable them to establish micro-enterprises and secure sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

To build self-reliant communities where every member has the resources, confidence, and opportunities to pursue a better quality of life.
